Affordances

An affordance is cue which gives a hint how users may interact with something, no matter physical or digital. I came up with four affordances for the stainless steel bottle. First, it can afford to hold any beverage/ liquid. Second, it can afford to keep things at any desired temperature. Third, it can afford to keep its surroundings clean. Lastly, it can afford the beverage/ liquid to be sipped or gulped.
